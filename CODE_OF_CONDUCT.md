# Code of Conduct

We are committed to a respectful and harassment-free community.

## Our standards

Examples of expected behavior:

- Be respectful and constructive in discussions and reviews
- Assume good intent and focus on technical issues
- Welcome people with different backgrounds and experience levels

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination
- Personal attacks, insults, or trolling
- Publishing private information without permission

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces, including issues, pull requests, discussions, and other community channels.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it to maintainers privately at:

- chaizhenhua@gmail.com

All reports will be reviewed and handled confidentially.
